- **Property Identification**: Property ID **PROP-016**, located in Damascus.
- **Buyer Identification**: Client ID **CLI-016**, Hassan Jaber, with national identification number **16**.

- **Contract Status**: The Agreement has been duly executed by both Parties and is considered legally binding.

### III. Legal Clauses and Conditions

#### 1. Payment Terms

- **Deposit and Payment Schedule**: The Buyer shall pay fifty percent (50%) of the total purchase price, equivalent to **$223,250 USD**, as an earnest deposit ("Deposit") upon execution of this Agreement. The remaining fifty percent (50%), amounting to **$223,250 USD**, shall be payable upon the transfer of the property deed.
- **Method of Payment**: All payments shall be made via bank transfer or other mutually agreed-upon secure payment methods. The Seller shall provide necessary payment instructions upon request.
