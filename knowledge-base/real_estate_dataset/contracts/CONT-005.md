# Legal Contract: CONT-005 - Sale Agreement

## 1. Contract Overview
This Sale Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Sham Real Estate Group ("Seller") and Sami Khoury ("Buyer") regarding the purchase of the property identified as Property ID: PROP-013, located in Homs ("Property"). The terms and conditions outlined herein are legally binding and govern the rights and obligations of both parties.

## 2. Contract Details
- **Contract Type:** Sale Agreement
- **Property Identification:** PROP-013, Homs
- **Buyer Identification:** Sami Khoury, Client ID: CLI-002
- **Agreed Purchase Price:** USD $373,350 (United States Dollars), as negotiated and agreed upon by both parties.
- **Contract Status:** Executed and binding upon signature of both parties.

## 3. Payment Terms and Conditions
### 3.1. Payment Schedule
The Buyer shall make payments in accordance with the following schedule:
- **Initial Payment:** Fifty percent (50%) of the total purchase price, amounting to USD $186,675, shall be paid upfront upon signing this Agreement.
- **Balance Payment:** The remaining fifty percent (50%), amounting to USD $186,675, shall be paid in full at the time of the transfer of title deed of the Property.

### 3.2. Payment Method
All payments shall be made via bank transfer or certified check to an account designated by the Seller. The Seller shall provide the necessary banking details upon execution of this Agreement.

### 3.3. Default and Remedies
Failure by the Buyer to comply with the payment schedule constitutes a material breach. In such cases, the Seller reserves the right to terminate this Agreement and retain any deposits paid as liquidated damages, without prejudice to other rights and remedies available under applicable law.

## 4. Liabilities and Disclaimers
### 4.1. Municipal Approvals and Permits
The Seller shall not be responsible for delays in obtaining municipal approvals, permits, or other regulatory clearances necessary for the transfer of ownership or use of the Property. The Buyer acknowledges that approval timelines are beyond the Seller’s control.

### 4.2. Due Diligence
The Buyer confirms that they have conducted or shall conduct all necessary due diligence regarding the Property, including but not limited to, title verification, zoning, and compliance with local regulations.

### 4.3. Limitation of Liability
The Seller shall not be liable for any damages, losses, or costs incurred by the Buyer arising from delays, municipal holds, or regulatory issues outside the Seller’s control. The Buyer accepts the Property in its current condition, "as is," with no warranties or representations made by the Seller regarding the Property's condition or suitability for any particular purpose.
