# Legal Contract: CONT-010 — Sale Agreement

## I. Contract Overview

This Sale Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Contract") is entered into between Sham Real Estate Group (hereinafter referred to as the "Seller") and Sami Khoury (ID: 2) (hereinafter referred to as the "Buyer") concerning the transfer of ownership of the property identified as Property ID: PROP-007, located in Homs. The terms outlined herein are legally binding and govern the rights and obligations of both parties.

## II. Contract Details

- **Contract Type:** Sale Agreement
- **Property Identification:** PROP-007 (Homs)
- **Buyer Identification:** Sami Khoury (ID: 2)
- **Agreed Purchase Price:** USD $92,150 (United States Dollars Ninety-Two Thousand One Hundred Fifty), as mutually negotiated and agreed upon by the Parties.
- **Contract Status:** Executed and in full force and effect.

## III. Legal Clauses and Obligations

### 1. Payment Terms

**1.1. Payment Schedule:**  
The Buyer shall pay the total purchase price of USD $92,150 according to the following schedule:  
- An initial deposit amounting to fifty percent (50%) of the purchase price, equivalent to USD $46,075, payable at the signing of this Contract.  
- The remaining fifty percent (50%), amounting to USD $46,075, shall be payable immediately upon the transfer of the deed and the official registration of the property in the Buyer's name.

**1.2. Method of Payment:**  
All payments shall be made via certified bank transfer or any other mutually agreed-upon method, with proof of payment provided to the Seller.

**1.3. Non-Payment and Default:**  
Failure by the Buyer to fulfill payment obligations within the stipulated timeframes shall constitute a breach of this Contract, entitling the Seller to pursue remedies including, but not limited to, termination of the Contract and retention of any deposits as liquidated damages.
