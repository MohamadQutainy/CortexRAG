## III. Legal Clauses and Binding Obligations

### 1. Payment Terms and Schedule
**1.1. Payment Structure:**  
The Buyer shall pay the Purchase Price of $395,200 USD in accordance with the following schedule:  
- An initial deposit representing fifty percent (50%) of the total purchase price, amounting to $197,600 USD, payable upon signing this Agreement ("Initial Payment").  
- The remaining fifty percent (50%), amounting to $197,600 USD, shall be payable upon the transfer of the Deed of Ownership ("Final Payment").

**1.2. Payment Methods:**  
All payments shall be made via bank transfer or certified check to the account designated by the Seller. Receipt of funds shall be acknowledged in writing.

**1.3. Failure to Pay:**  
In the event of non-payment of any installment within the stipulated timeframe, the Seller shall have the right to terminate this Agreement, retain any deposits as liquidated damages, and pursue any other legal remedies available.
