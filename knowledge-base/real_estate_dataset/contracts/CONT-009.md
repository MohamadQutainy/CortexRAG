This Sale Agreement (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") constitutes a legally binding contract between Sham Real Estate Group (hereinafter referred to as "Seller") and Ms. Sarah Khalil (hereinafter referred to as "Buyer"), relating to the transfer of ownership of the property identified as Property ID: PROP-016, located in Damascus.

## II. Legal Clauses and Parties' Obligations

### 1. Payment Terms and Conditions
- **Deposit and Payment Schedule**: The Buyer shall remit fifty percent (50%) of the total purchase price, amounting to USD $223,250, as an initial deposit ("Deposit") within five (5) business days from the execution of this Agreement.
- **Final Payment**: The remaining fifty percent (50%), amounting to USD $223,250, shall be payable upon the successful transfer of the property deed to the Buyer, but no later than the date of deed transfer.
- **Payment Method**: All payments shall be made via bank transfer to the Seller’s designated account, details of which shall be provided separately.
- **Default and Remedies**: In the event that the Buyer fails to comply with the payment schedule, the Seller reserves the right to terminate this Agreement, retain any deposits as liquidated damages, and pursue applicable legal remedies.
